Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

AntiShorts ("the Extension") is a Safari Web Extension developed by Prodius. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

The Extension operates entirely on your device. It does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies
  • Include any third-party SDKs that collect data

How the Extension Works

The Extension uses three local mechanisms to block YouTube Shorts content:

  • CSS rules injected into YouTube pages to hide Shorts-related UI elements
  • JavaScript that runs locally in the browser to redirect /shorts/ URLs and observe page changes
  • Declarative net request rules processed by Safari to redirect Shorts URLs before the page loads

All processing happens on your Mac. No data leaves your device.

Local Storage

The Extension stores a single boolean preference (enabled/disabled toggle state) using the browser's local storage API. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Permissions

The Extension requests access to youtube.com to function. It does not request or require access to any other websites.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Since we do not collect any data, changes are unlikely.
