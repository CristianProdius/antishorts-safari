Support

Having trouble with AntiShorts? Find answers below or reach out to us.

Getting Started

  1. Install the app from the Mac App Store
  2. Open Safari → Settings → Extensions
  3. Check the box next to "AntiShorts"
  4. Grant permission for youtube.com when prompted
  5. Visit youtube.com — Shorts will be hidden

Frequently Asked Questions

The extension isn't blocking Shorts

Make sure the extension is enabled in Safari → Settings → Extensions and that you've granted it permission for youtube.com. Try reloading the YouTube page. Also check the extension popup in the Safari toolbar — the toggle should show "Blocking Shorts".

How do I temporarily disable blocking?

Click the extension icon in the Safari toolbar and toggle the switch off. Reload the YouTube page to see Shorts again. Toggle it back on anytime.

Does this extension work on iPhone or iPad?

Not currently. This is a macOS-only Safari extension. iOS support may be considered in a future version.

Does the extension collect my data?

No. The extension runs entirely on your Mac and does not collect, store, or transmit any data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Some Shorts still appear momentarily

YouTube loads content dynamically. The extension uses multiple techniques to catch Shorts elements as they appear, but there may be a brief flash before they are hidden. This is normal and the elements will be removed within milliseconds.

The extension stopped working after a YouTube update

YouTube occasionally changes its page structure. If the extension stops working, please report the issue on GitHub and we will release an update.
